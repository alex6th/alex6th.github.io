Macbeth

A gripping performance of Shakespeare's timeless tragedy

About the Performance

Join us for a dramatic retelling of Macbeth, performed by Bob Smith and a talented cast of young actors, all from Farnborough Sixth Form. This modern adaptation explores ambition, fate, and madness through powerful performances and striking visuals.
